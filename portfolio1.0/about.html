About

Bree is a web developer and designer, currently living in Detroit, Michigan. She enjoy's blending creative communication design, and technological development. Transforming wireframes into beautiful responsive websites is her passion. She strives to build immersive and clean sites, using clean code and user-interface design practices.She has skills in multiple graphic design programs such as Sketch and Adobe Creative Suite. As well as knowledge of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, Git, UI Design, UX Design, and Responsive Website Development.

Outside of work you can find Bree walking through a nature preserve with her son and dog. Possibly binge watching the Star Wars Saga, Rick & Morty, or at a football game cheering on the Michigan Wolverines.
